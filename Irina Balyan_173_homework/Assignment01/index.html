EUROPA STAMP

In 1956, the 6 European nations simultaneously issued the first EUROPA stamps thus reflecting the desire for closer cooperation and integration in Europe bulding awareness of the common roots, culture and history. This first "Joint Stamp Issue" marked the birth of the most popular and collected stamp in the world - EUROPA stamp.

In 1993, PostEurop, which is the Association of European Postal Operators and has 48 European members, became responsibe for issuing EUROPA stamps. Every year a theme for issuing stamps is choesen and a competition among the stamps issued is held.

PARTICIPATION OF ARMENIA

Armenia, as a member od PostEurop, has been also issuing EUROPA stamps. And it is worth mentioning that in 2007 the stamp dedicated to 100 years of scoutism and issued bu Armenia, was selected as the best EUROPA stamps in 2007.

ARMENIA AT EUROPA STAMP 2010

This year the theme is "Children's Books" and Armenia issued a stamp with picture of Irina Balyan, reflecting "Songs of love",(2008) of Nahapet Kuchak, a famous Armenian Poet.
